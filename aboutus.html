ABOUT US

At Thrifty, we champion sustainable fashion to reduce the industry's environmental footprint. Our mission is to provide high-quality, affordable upcycled clothing. By promoting a circular economy, we give clothes a second life instead of letting them end up in landfills. We build trust and transparency through our consignment services, fostering strong relationships with consignors. Our detailed process showcases items to eco-conscious shoppers, with regular updates and clear communication, ensuring your items are in good hands as we work toward a sustainable fashion future.

Prioritizing hygiene and quality, we thoroughly wash all clothing before sale using eco-friendly detergents and gentle methods.
